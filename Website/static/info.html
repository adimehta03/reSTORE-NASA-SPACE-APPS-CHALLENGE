WORKFLOW

Know us better!

1. MEASURE

We use an air quality sensor to measure the quality of air around you.



2. RECEIVE

This Air quality index is sent to our servers and fed to our model, which inturn recommends the next course of action



3. RECOMMEND

We recommend air emission plants(air pollutant absorbing plants) which absorb the air pollutants as shown by the air quality sensor and an ML model which recommends you the best form of green life taking into consideration some valid environmental factors around the living space of an individual.



4. BUILD

These plants can then be vertically planted mainly in corporate offices. Also we intend to provide light sensors for plant growth indoor, watering facilities with drip irrigators, RO purifiers for reusability and pest repellent plants for maintainance.
